About Us!

PokeOne is a fan-made free-to-play MMO that aims to provide a platform for players to feel like they're playing a traditional Pokemon game, but with their friends! PokeOne is updated to the latest 7th Generation Moves, Abilities, and Mechanics of Pokemon. PokeOne currently has the Kanto and Johto Regions, with Unova currently in development. For more information, join us on our Discord Server, or you know... Play the game ;)
